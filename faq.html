Impact

To infinity and beyond!!! Our cups could theoretically be used thousands of times before reaching the end of their life. But we know that some cups will break, get lost, or fogotten in someone’s desk.
Our goal is to achieve an average of 1000 uses per cup, and to do so we will need your help! Make sure you leave your cup in one of our bins, and give the cup the long life it deserves. 👵🏻

(PS: to calculate our impact, though, we use the research benchmark of only 500 uses per cup)

Worldwide, more than 500 billion single-use cups are wasted every year, almost half are plastic, with only a 9% of recycling rate! The carbon footprint can amount to a whopping 26 billion tons of CO2eq. emmisions. That’s more 2 times the pollution of all Ryanair operations and flights!

Sustainability is our driving force, and we are on a long-term mission to rid the world of single use packaging. To do so, we use durable Polypropylene (PP) cups, produced by local partners to minimize transportation emmissions. These cups, throughout their lifespan emit much less greenhouse gases than the other options. In fact, our cups, per each use, emit just:
  • 48% of recycled paper cup emissions
  • 31% of landfilled paper cup emissions
  • 17% of recycled plastic cup emissions
  • 10% of landfilled plastic cup emissions
If we were to serve all vending machines in the EU, we could save up to 1 million tons of CO2 per year!
