General

Loopack is a pioneer in solving the disposable problem, once and for all!
Our amazing circular IoT system creates an effortless solution for reusing coffee cups: just buy your drink, drink it, throw the cup away and we ensure the cup will be used again!

Glad you ask! We integrate an awesome system in vending machines, to dispense one of our reusable cups. In that moment, we can associate the cup to your payment details. Once you finish drinking, you just throw the cup in one of our smart bins (which will be as common as recycling bins) and we will reward you!

We know, sounds unbelievable! But yes! Actually, we will pay you directly. That’s how much we love you. Just make sure you register your student card information on our app, and give your MBWay/ Revolut or Paypal info (we’ll do only one transfer per month, though, cause we do fear the transfer limits on MBway). We can also give you a code for a free coffee in the vending machine!

Hell yeah! Come to the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (for now), we are running a pilot on the vending machines of the Department of Electronics and Informatics (building I). Read the rest of the FAQ for some more practical stuff.

We have been known by many names: green vigilantes, technology ninjas, silent knights of innovation... and coffee addicts (I can stop if I want, I swear)!
We are a bit like unicorns, but with fewer rainbows, ensuring you have a magical sustainability experience, filled with more smiles than a clown car at a circus!

Practical

Time to use your university/student card. Just follow this steps 1. (optional) for a better experience register your profile in our app. But first you need to find out your card’s NFC number. It’s easy jus follow this steps (link to explanation) 2. Come to building I, just pass the card on our dispenser, take the cup and get yout coffee. 3. After drinking it drop it in one of our bins! 4. Smile, enjoy the fact you just helped the planet, and for an added bonus follow us on instagram/ linkedin, and tag us in a story of you using the system! (double your reward for a whole week!)
